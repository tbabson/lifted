Lifted 2023

LIFTED is the first program of the youth wing of Ogun Province 30 in the redeemed new calendar year. The sole aim of this program is to lead the youths to God, stay in His presence and be blessed through the ministration of word, prayer and worship.

We believe that the Almighty God will draw everyone closer to himself as you come to experience the presence of God in Jesus' Name.
